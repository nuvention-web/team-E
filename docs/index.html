Team E

Team Mission

Our mission is to match undergraduate students, particularly freshmen, with the academic resources on campus that will help them succeed.

Our Team Goals

  1. Create a product that reflects the best effort of every team member.
  2. Create a product that users find intuitive to use.
  3. Create a product that users find beneficial in their regular routine.

Product Space

Our product space focuses on undergraduate students on campus, particularly underclassmen, who do not use the academic resources available to them. These resources are underused in part because there is no central portal where students can find them. There is also the problem of the stigma associated with needing help and of perceived ineffectivness of academic resources.

Target Users

Our target users are undergraduates, particularly freshman and other new students.
