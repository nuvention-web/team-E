Team E
Team Mission

Our team mission is to help users in our product space work efficiently and utilize their research and data to tell stories using data and analytics.

Our Team Goals
  1. Create a product that reflects the best effort of every team member.
  2. Create a product that users find intuitive to use.
  3. Create a product that users find beneficial in their regular routine.

Product Space

Our product space focuses on the productivity of a mobile user, with a specific target audience of data journalists. Our teamâ€™s goal is to increase the ease with which data journalists visualize and understand data and information. We aim to increase productivity in the industry of data journalism by streamlining their data comprehension processes through the use of our mobile and/or web tools.

Target Users

Our target users are journalists looking to use numerical data and visuals to produce and distribute stories and information. We are looking to work with journalists in the Northwestern University Knight Lab and other journalism students on campus to assess their needs and tools that would aid their productivity.
